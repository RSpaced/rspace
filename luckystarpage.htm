Did you know I like Lucky Star?

(I wouldn't call myself a hardcore otaku, but I am somewhat one. I deliberately bought the DVD/Blu-Ray combo boxset of Lucky Star just to own it (I already have digital versions) and I've watched Your Name and Weathering With You (I now own the DVDs of both!), among other things (i'm still getting into all of this in general)) BUT THAT'S BESIDE THE POINT; THIS PAGE IS ABOUT LUCKY STAR!

Lucky Star is the king of Moe in terms of anime. I mean have you SEEN  Tsukasa? Tsukasa is probably the biggest 'meatball' character in terms of anime and I love her for it, she's just so precious... ANYWAY basically I really, really like this anime and honestly i can keep rewatching it on and on and on and on and on etc. It was what got me into anime in general and now I'm really happy where I am, also not to mention the DATING SIM on PSP??! (I know it's on PS2 as well but still) 

One of the reasons I bought a PSP in the first place was to play Ryouou Gakuen Otousai (and Ridge Racer, I ought to show off my collection of stuff at some point... spoiler for future webpage stuff :)

I will buy the character CDs at some point physically. Oh wait. Did I forget to mention the character CDs?

Please watch Lucky Star. It's actually great. If 17 years of youtube lucky star clips (and the Kirby remix) haven't convinced you, then you should go see a doctor or something idk
