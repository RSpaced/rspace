So you know how I said I'd give my AIM screen name in the last blog post? I bet you're wondering 'I thought AIM shut down years ago!', and in any case you'd be right. But in the spirit of Pretendo and many other server revival projects, AOL Instant Messenger is back!

It's called nina.chat and I signed up to it a few days ago. I wasn't, well... alive while AIM was in its heyday but I've always wanted to relive that form of Instant Messenger type thing as an alternative to Discord occasionally. Setup was really simple as well, so I'd recommend going to their website and giving it a go. I don't know whether AIM 5.9 still works on newer versions of Windows (It works on my ThinkPad T42 running Windows XP) but there's probably a workaround to get it running.
